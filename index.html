About me !

I love the train. On a rainy day, I imagine raindrops racing one another as they trail down the window, and in the summer, I like observing the different layers of the horizon passing by, each at its own speed. I eat mashed potatoes with the back of my fork, like a cornfield being harvested. I always sneeze in pairs, I dance in the supermarket when no one’s around, and I collect empty jars “just in case.” I like to imagine people living in tall buildings with windows lit up by different shades of yellow. I rewatch the same movies if I like them, but hate to read a book for the second time.

Growing up, I had a fascination with making things out of cardboard, an obsession with penguins, I dreamed of moving to Alaska, spent hours teaching my chickens how to jump, and was convinced the mountain I saw from my garden was observing me.

Now, I do crosswords at the mere sight of the sun. When I’m bored, I wander the aisles of the grocery store searching for something new to try. I drink coffee in the morning and tea in the evening, and I get happy when I see the driver tap their finger on the steering wheel to the rhythm of the song I put on the AUX.
